Western Australian Shipwrecks Dashboard

Explore records and details for over 1650 Western Australian shipwrecks using our interactive dashboard. Follow the steps below to start your journey:

1. Select a Region: Begin by navigating to the "Shipwreck Region" dropdown menu located on the left side of the dashboard. Choose a region of interest to filter the shipwreck data.

2. Select a Shipwreck: Once you have selected a region, move to the "Shipwreck Name" dropdown menu below the region selector. Select a specific shipwreck to view detailed information.

3. View Shipwreck Details: Upon selecting a shipwreck, the "Ship Info" section will update to display key details about the shipwreck, including its name, the date it was lost, the location where it was lost, its latitude and longitude coordinates, the country where it was built, and a URL link (if available).

Use the map to explore the exact location of the shipwreck. A marker will be placed on the map at the shipwreck's coordinates, and clicking on the marker will display a popup with the shipwreck's name and location details.
